Plastic VR Module

Description

Additive manufacturing (AM) or 3D-Printing is the process of creating a 3D part via layer-by-layer material being fused together. In this project, a Virtual Reality (VR) module (headset-controlled) and an identical PC version (mouse & keyboard controlled) were developed in order to improve students’ learning of AM machines and processes. The PC version helps mitigate the negative effects some users experience when using a VR headset. The variation of this our modules explore is Fused Filament Fabrication (FFF - a.k.a. FDM), where thermoplastic based filament is fed into a “hot end” where it is melted while being extruded onto a build surface.
